About
the Company

Our Name, Our Story
GatherStar comes from two simple ideas: “gathering” and “stars.” Every premium advertiser (a “star” seeking engaged audiences) and publisher (a “star” with high-quality traffic) brings unique value to digital advertising.
GatherStar is here to connect these “stars” on one unified platform, acting as their bridge to match advertiser stars with traffic stars that make their work count. Together, they shine brighter.
The Problem We Solve
We built GatherStar because advertisers and publishers were stuck in silos. Advertisers, particularly those in niche industries, fought to find reliable, high-intent traffic; publishers struggled to connect with brands that valued their traffic. This fragmentation wasted opportunities for both sides, so we created a unified space to bridge that divide.
What We Do Today
Our goal is to lead global programmatic advertising and set standards for quality, transparency, and innovation. We keep it simple: help advertisers find their ideal audience, and help publishers monetize their traffic more efficiently, all through one platform designed to connect the right partners.
Because at its core, GatherStar is a programmatic advertising platform that connects advertisers and publishers to drive unlimited growth. And one truth always holds: Better matches = brighter results.

Our
Values

Partnership
We exclusively collaborate with verified traffic sources. This enables us to secure premium traffic placement for our partners and foster long-lasting relationships built on mutual trust.
Adaptability

Honesty
We adhere to the principle of honesty in all our transactions. This commitment allows us to maintain our reputation as a reliable partner.
