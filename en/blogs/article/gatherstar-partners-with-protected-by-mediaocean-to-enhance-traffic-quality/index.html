GatherStar Partners with Protected by Mediaocean to Enhance Traffic Quality


GatherStar, a global programmatic advertising platform that connects advertisers and publishers to drive unlimited growth, has secured a strategic partnership with Protected by Mediaocean, a leading ad verification solution provider under the Mediaocean family. The collaboration will integrate Protected by Mediaocean's industry-accredited ad fraud detection and traffic verification tools into GatherStar’s platform, marking a key step in GatherStar’s commitment to setting high standards for quality and transparency in programmatic advertising.

Partnership Delivers Verified Traffic for Advertisers' High-Stakes Campaigns

For advertisers leveraging GatherStar's platform, this partnership ensures ad spend reaches real audiences in brand-safe environments. Protected by Mediaocean, which holds accreditations from the Media Rating Council (MRC) for viewability and Sophisticated Invalid Traffic (SIVT) detection, will provide real-time verification of GatherStar’s ad inventory across desktop, mobile web, mobile in-app, and connected TV (CTV) channels.

This means advertisers running campaigns with GatherStar can access 100% verified traffic, filtered of bot activity, fake impressions, and low-quality placements. By eliminating redundant spending on invalid traffic, GatherStar helps advertisers boost return on investment (ROI) while ensuring ads align with brand safety guidelines.

Boosting Quality for Publishers, Too: A Win-for-All Ecosystem

The collaboration also benefits GatherStar's premium publishers. Protected by Mediaocean's verification will validate the authenticity of publishers' audience data, helping high-quality publishers stand out and attract more advertiser demand, ultimately increasing their eCPM (effective cost per mille) and revenue potential.

By weeding out low-quality or fraudulent traffic sources, GatherStar ensures that publishers with genuine, engaged audiences are rewarded, while maintaining the platform's reputation as a trusted hub for premium inventory.

A Step Toward Transparent, Quality-Driven Programmatic Advertising

This partnership between GatherStar and Protected by Mediaocean is a reaffirmation of GatherStar's vision to "lead programmatic advertising, setting industry standards for quality, transparency, and innovation." For advertisers, publishers, and the industry at large, this is a concrete example of how combining technology and accountability can build a programmatic landscape where every impression is trusted, every budget is efficient, and every stakeholder wins.
