GatherStar, an AI-powered DSP programmatic advertising platform, is heading to Affiliate World Asia 2025, the world’s #1 performance marketing conference.  

Event Details

Date: December 3–4, 2025  

Venue: Centara Grand and Bangkok Convention Center (Level 22, 999/9 Rama 1 Road, Pathumwan, Bangkok 10330 Thailand)  

Scale: 7,000+ attendees from 110+ countries, featuring 120+ advertisers, 40+ traffic sources, and 170+ affiliate networks  

Official Website: https://affiliateworldconferences.com/asia  

Why Talk to GatherStar?

For Advertisers: Acquire High-Intent Global Users

  • Global Reach: Access premium traffic across 180+ countries, with a focus on high-growth regions including the U.S., Southeast Asia, Russia, Latin America, and the Middle East.

  • Flexible Pricing Models: Choose from CPM, CPI, or CPA to align with your business objectives.

For Publishers: Monetize Inventory Efficiently

  • Maximize Earnings: Unlock the full value of your ad space by matching with high-intent advertisers and optimizing revenue potential.

  • Streamlined Operations: We simplify workflows—from ad requests to payouts—so you can focus on growing your platform.
