Marketplace

Viewing sites and zones available for advertising

The Marketplace shows list of all traffic sources within the network, including the available ad spots for each website, along with their respective Daily Impressions and Clicks.  

Note: Traffic source data reflects activity from two days prior. For RTB zones to appear in the marketplace, they must have a minimum of 1,000 queries.

 

You can filter the results by Category, Traffic Type, Ad Format, Ad Placement (Footer, NTV, etc.) and Device Type. You can also search specific entries based on the Site Name, Site ID or Zone ID. Not all the ad zones have an ad placement assigned to them.

Note: Please note that you need to be logged into your account to see the Marketplace tab. The Marketplace tab will show using the currency you selected when you set up your account. You cannot change the currency on an existing account.

 

Blacklisted Sites

 

With the feature of blocking sites on an account level, you cannot buy an ad spot from the sites that are blacklisted. You will see a warning icon highlighted in red before the BUY button and this button will be greyed out. On the hover of this icon, the following message is displayed: This site has been blacklisted on your account.

 

Buying an ad zone in the Marketplace tab

You can click Buy on the right side of any ad zone in the list to buy it. You will be taken to the Campaign tab, where you can fill in the rest of the details of the campaign that will target this ad zone.
